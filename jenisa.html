About Dr. Shrestha

Dr. Shrestha is a highly skilled physician specializing in both family medicine and emergency medicine. Her diverse expertise allows her to provide comprehensive care across a wide range of medical needs, making her a versatile and invaluable asset to her patients and the community.

After completing medical school, Dr. Shrestha completed family medicine training. Along with that, she completed fellowship training in lifestyle medicine. She further honed her skills by pursuing specialized fellowship training in emergency medicine, equipping her to handle urgent and critical medical situations with precision and efficiency. This dual expertise enables Dr. Shrestha to offer a unique blend of preventive care and acute medical intervention.

With a patient-centered approach, Dr. Shrestha is dedicated to fostering long-term relationships with her patients, ensuring continuity of care through all stages of life. Her practice is rooted in the belief that effective healthcare is not only about treating illnesses but also about educating and empowering patients to lead healthier lives.

In her role as an emergency medicine specialist, Dr. Shrestha thrives in high-pressure environments, delivering swift and accurate medical care during critical situations. Her calm demeanor and decisive action are reassuring to both patients and colleagues alike.

Outside of her professional commitments, Dr. Shrestha is actively involved in community outreach and health education programs. She is passionate about raising awareness and providing resources that promote health and wellness within the community.

Dr. Shrestha's commitment to excellence in both family and emergency medicine makes her a trusted healthcare provider who is deeply dedicated to improving the well-being of her patients.
