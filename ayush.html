About Dr. Shrestha

Dr. Shrestha is a dual board-certified physician with specialized training in both primary care and sports medicine, offering comprehensive care for patients of all ages. With a passion for helping individuals stay active and healthy, he brings a unique combination of expertise in musculoskeletal health, preventive care, and chronic disease management.

Dr. Shrestha began his medical journey in 2006 and continued residency training in Orthopedic Surgery, followed by a prestigious Fellowship in Hand Surgery at the University of Louisville. His desire to provide more holistic, long-term care to patients led him to pursue further training in Family Medicine at Michigan State University, and a Fellowship in Sports Medicine at Southern Illinois University.

In his practice, Dr. Shrestha blends the latest medical advances with a patient-centered approach. He performs a wide range of in-office procedures for both primary care and sports medicine, including joint injections, ultrasound-guided treatments, minor surgeries, and regenerative therapies. Whether treating a student athlete, weekend warrior, or a senior managing chronic joint pain, Dr. Shrestha provides thoughtful, effective care tailored to each individual.
